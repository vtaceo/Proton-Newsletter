Dear Proton community member,
Millions of people use Proton Mail and Proton Calendar every day to communicate and plan their lives. This is a massive step forward for privacy online, as each new member of the Proton community is removing their information from Big Tech and state surveillance.
We’re constantly adding new features to make it easier for even more people to protect their privacy with our inbox and calendar. Here’s a collection of recent Proton Mail and Proton Calendar updates you might have missed.
New official badge against phishing attacks
To make it easier for you to identify emails from Proton, we’ve added an official badge to Proton Mail with the latest update of our apps (web, iPhone/iPad, and Android).
Going forward, all legitimate emails from Proton will come with this official badge and will no longer be automatically starred.
How Proton Mail protects you from phishing
Proton Mail’s PhishGuard prevents phishing attempts from ever reaching your inbox, blocking over 130,000 messages daily. Emails that pass PhishGuard but don’t pass all our authentication checks come with a notification banner so you know to review them carefully.
You can also enable link confirmation, which allows you to inspect the destination URL of links in your emails to further enhance your security. Remember to always exercise caution if you come across a suspicious email.
You can read more about phishing attacks and how Proton helps prevent them on our blog.
→ Learn more about phishing attacks
Send emails when you want with Schedule send
Timing is everything when you send an email. If you get it right, you’ll receive a quick response. If you get it wrong, your message might get lost in your recipient’s inbox.
We recently introduced Schedule send to our web and iPhone apps. Now you can draft emails whenever is convenient for you and have them delivered whenever is convenient for your recipient.
To get started on the web, click the arrow next to the send button. On your phone, tap the clock icon when drafting a message.
→ Learn more about Schedule send
Quickly check your calendar and manage contacts with the side panel
With the new side panel, you can view your inbox, calendar, and contacts all in one window without switching tabs.
The side panel lets you easily manage your schedule by allowing you to see your inbox and calendar side by side. You can also use it to access your contacts to quickly start conversations.
Select the calendar or contacts icon at the top right corner of your inbox to get started.
→ Learn more about the side panel

Get instant access to your favorite email actions with the new customizable toolbar on the Proton Mail app for iPhone. From quickly labeling emails to saving them as PDFs, you can customize the toolbar to suit your preferences. To get started, go to settings and tap Customize toolbar.
